Every year we recycle more than 1.5 millions mattresses and divert more than 52 million pounds of useful material from the waste stream.

The Mattress Recicling Council is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the ongoing development and implementation of statewide mattress reclycling programs for states that hace enacted laws.

Current Statewide Recycling Programs. Several states across the nation have enacted mattress recyclng legislation. If you're doing business in California, Connecticut or Rhode Island, click your state to learn how the law affects you and how to recycle your mattresses and box springs at no cost.
